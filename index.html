OUR MISSION

With the recent migrant crisis impacting Europe and the world on an unprecedented scale, our mission is to contribute to the process of integration of refugees into these communities through innovation and technology. Refugees face a number of tough challenges once their asylum status has been obtained, but one of the most critical and most burdensome problems for European nations medium to longer term will be that of economic integration through job opportunity. Our organization aims to tackle this by providing refugees with opportunities to learn and connect with through computer literacy and code. Through action and example, we hope to break down stereotypes about these individuals and highlight their potential for contributing to their host country.

OUR CORE VALUES

Inclusion

We welcome everyone of all ages and backgrounds into our community! Our initiatives are based on an inclusive and innovative model of learning that fosters exchange between participants. Our students-approximately 50% refugees and 50% local - learn together side by side, gaining not only technical skills, but also an exchange of different cultures, languages and friendships that accelerates the inclusion of refugees into their new host community.

Interchange

We encourage active participation and contribution from each and every individual. Former participants have come to give back to beginning students. Some of our teaching assistants are developers who are refugees themselves, including two team members who have helped to build our organization website.

Partnership

We believe firmly in building a community through close partnerships on the ground. With growing partnerships and a dedicated team, Konexio aims to first empower our students with technical skills and next support them in the launch of their career aspirations-through mentorship, network, and professional development resources that can train them on the social codes essential to navigating the pro¬fessional world successfully in their host country.

Objective of our organisation as as follows:

tech fundamental concept of web development through easy, interective methods and onlined platforms

empower our participants with professional opportunities

build a supportive community of individuals passionate about code and engaged in the tech community

OUR INITIATIVES

We have two main programs for skill-building: digital skills which focus on building up skills in computer literacy and digital communication, and code skills which focus on building skills in web development and programming

SKILL-BUILDING PROGRAMS

DIGITAL SKILLS

Developed in line with Digital Competence standards recognized at the European level and in reflection of the current demands for digital skills today, our digital skills program consists of a sequence of cumulative courses covering basic computer use to internet navigation and word processing/spreadsheet proficiency

Do you want to gain valuable computer literacy skills to improve your professional skills or facilitate tasks in your daily life? Ready to learn and make new friends? Then sign up here to enroll in our digital skills program.

APPRENTICESHIP

In addition to teaching our students key technical skills, we want to connect them with their first professional paid opportunities, which is incredibly powerful in boosting their confidence and getting them a foot in the door to the professional world. For students who have completed our skill-building programs, we connect them with a range of professional activities, from microwork experiences sourced from partner platforms to small-scale freelance projects sourced from independent clients. This allows our students to continue their learning while gaining experience and projects to showcase in their portfolio. More information forthcoming
