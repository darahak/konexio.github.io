Our Mission

With the recent migrant crisis impacting Europe and the world on an unprecedented scale, our mission is to contribute to the process of integration of refugees into these communities through innovation and technology. Refugees face a number of tough challenges once their asylum status has been obtained, but one of the most critical and most burdensome problems for European nations medium to longer term will be that of economic integration through job opportunity. Our organization aims to tackle this by providing refugees with opportunities to learn and connect with through code.

Our Core Values

Inclusion

We welcome everyone of all ages and backgrounds into our community! Our initiatives are based on an inclusive and innovative model of learning that fosters exchange between participants. Our students—approximately 50% refugees and 50% local—learn together side by side, gaining not only technical skills, but also an exchange of different cultures, languages and friendships that accelerates the inclusion of refugees into their new host community.

Interchange

We encourage active participation and contribution from each and every individual. Former participants have come to give back to beginning students. Some of our teaching assistants are developers who are refugees themselves, including two team members who have helped to build our organization website.

Partnership

We believe firmly in building a community through close partnerships on the ground. With growing partnerships and a dedicated team, Konexio aims to first empower our students with technical skills and next support them in the launch of their career aspirations—through mentorship, network, and professional development resources that can inform them on the social codes essential to navigating the professional world successfully in their host country.

Our Objectives

  • Teach fundamental concepts of computer literacy, web development and programming through interactive methods promoting exchange between students
  • Empower our participants with professional opportunities
  • Build a supportive community of individuals passionate about code and engaged in the tech community

Our Activities

Workshops

We give free regular workshops several evenings per week for 4-6 week sessions. The sessions start with basic concepts of web development—moving from HTML and CSS to Javascript and beyond, and make use of interactive online platforms and exercises adapted for participants of all backgrounds and learning abilities.

Want to learn the concepts necessary to create a website, discover the foundations of programming, and connect with others passionate about coding? Then come participate in our free workshops! Please fill out the questionnaire in the link provided here, and we will follow up with you for more details!

Project Lab

Personalized lab for students who have completed the beginner workshops and need more focused attention for their personal projects.

Mentor Program

Mentorship pairing between participants and professionals that includes formal and informal guidance on developing personal projects, setting career goals, and navigating professionally in the host community.
